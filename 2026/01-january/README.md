# January 2026

Google platform agent suite. Three independent agents for Google's major platforms.

## What Shipped

**google-ads-agent** (private)
Google Ads campaign management agent. Full campaign lifecycle - creation, optimization,
reporting - accessible via natural language.

**google-cloud-agent** (private)
Google Cloud Platform management agent. Infrastructure and service management
through an agent interface.

**google-youtube-agent** (private)
YouTube channel management agent. Content operations, analytics, and channel
management via agent.

---

_Each agent is an independent repo with its own MCP interface. Pattern established here
became the blueprint for GHL toolkit architecture._
